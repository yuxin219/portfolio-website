Due to the Coronavirus outbreak, students' classes changed to online classes, they stay at home and use an APP called "Zoom" for attending classes. Students form elementary school to colleges are using zoom, also there are students in other nations who are using this APP. I felt like students from other nations in all ages are in one same university, the "Zoom University". This poster was inspired by the COVID-19 pandemic and the switch to online classes. It highlights the way students use Zoom: not turning their video on, and using their pets as their avatar. These are what I saw during my online classes.
